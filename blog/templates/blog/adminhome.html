ARA INTELLIGENT PARKING SOLUTIONS



The smart way to park


ARA Intelligent Parking Management System makes use of the full potential of digitalization: smart sensors, intelligent software, and clever and neat analysis of the available data. The system eliminates the development of parking-related traffic and ensures efficient and optimum utilization of urban parking facilities. This saves time, reduces the impact of traffic on the environment and improves the quality of life in urban cities.

Suspicious Activity Detection


The ARA Intelligent Parking Management System has been developed for public safety as it is capable of detecting suspicious people or activities in realtime. On the basis of lightweight human object detection and tracking algorithms, the ARA Intelligent Parking Management Systema advances proactive surveillance system design through SVAS which can be deployed with respect to automatic recognition and interpretation of composite events. This event detection procedure comprises of mainly two levels, low level and high level. The results obtained from low level can be used to analyze people behavior and aid in object detection which can be further used for high level analysis that is event detection.

  Parking Ticket Dispensing


ARA Intelligent Parking Management System retrieves the time-stamp of the vehicle from the repositiory which was alloted to the vehicle at the entrance. Using the time-stamp, the fare is calculated and the parking ticket is dispensed at the exit point. This process removes any human interaction thus eliminating any human error factor.
